Itinerary Highlights

Kamakura Serenity

Forest bathing in Jōgashima’s sacred groves and a private tea ceremony.

Beppu Hot Springs

Sand, mud and steam baths plus a complimentary 60‑minute treatment.

Miyajima Island

Stay at a historic ryokan with kaiseki dinners and a view of the floating torii gate.

Tokyo Couture

Custom kimono styling session with Senanu and shopping in Ginza’s boutiques.

Kyoto Tranquility

Forest meditation and calligraphy workshop in a Zen temple.

Healing Waters & Forest Rituals

Experience private onsens, forest bathing and autumnal rituals designed to nurture and restore. Our journey transports you to warm springs and serene groves where you reconnect with yourself and your community.

Meet Dr. Dédé

Your guide and host, Dr. Dédé, has lived in Japan for 15 years. She curates transformative journeys for Black women reclaiming rest as a radical act. A licensed practitioner and cultural connector, she introduces you to off‑the‑beaten‑path rituals and sacred spaces.

Senanu Kimono

Our luxury kimonos are handmade by Senanu, blending African wax prints with Japanese silk. Your VIP package includes a custom yukata. Visit our Facebook and Instagram pages to explore more.
