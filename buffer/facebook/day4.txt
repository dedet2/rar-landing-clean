Meet Dr. Dédé

Healer, scholar and long‑time Japan resident, Dr. Dédé will guide you through every step of our restorative journey. For 15 years she’s blended Black feminist rest practices with Japanese hospitality and ritual. Through her fashion label Senanu she designs bespoke kimonos blending African wax prints and Japanese silk. On the trip you’ll receive a custom fitting!
