Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who is this retreat designed for?

Our journey centers Black women seeking a restorative blend of Japanese wellness traditions and Black liberation practices. All identities are welcome to apply.

What’s included in the price?

Lodging, intercity transport, daily onsens, workshops, curated meals and concierge support. Airfare, visas, insurance and add‑ons are extra.

How do I secure my spot?

Click “Pay Deposit” under your preferred journey to submit your deposit via Stripe, or submit an inquiry below.

Is onsen access inclusive and private?

We prioritize privacy and comfort. We arrange private or low‑exposure bathing options where possible and offer etiquette guidance.

Can I come solo?

Absolutely. Many guests attend solo and find deep community and care within the group.
