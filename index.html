Healing Waters & Forest Rituals

Shichirigahama Coastal Onsen

Private oceanside thermal baths where the healing mineral waters meet breathtaking Pacific views. Includes exclusive evening bathing under the stars.

Beppu’s Legendary “Hells”

Experience Japan’s most famous hot spring city with private access to the renowned thermal pools. Includes a specialized mud treatment ritual.

Iwaso Ryokan’s Sacred Waters

Centuries‑old healing waters on sacred Miyajima Island, complemented by traditional tea ceremony and restorative herbal infusions.

Forest Bathing & Rituals

Guided silent walks through ancient forests, breathwork beneath maple canopies, nature journaling and sunrise meditation among bamboo groves—carefully designed to reduce stress and honour ancestral connections to the natural world.
