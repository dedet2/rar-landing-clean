Meet Dr. Dédé

Dear friend,

Allow me to introduce myself. I’m Dr. Dédé, a healer, scholar and long‑time Japan resident. For over 15 years I’ve guided visitors off the beaten path and braided Black feminist rest practices with Japanese hospitality and ritual.

Through my fashion label, Senanu, I design bespoke kimonos blending African wax prints and Japanese silk. In our retreat you’ll receive a custom fitting and learn kimono etiquette adapted to our bodies and hair.

As your host, I’ll provide cultural translation with care—from ryokan etiquette to consent‑centered onsen guidance. Together we’ll create spacious opt‑outs and gentle pacing.
